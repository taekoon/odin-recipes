My Scrambled Egg Recipe

Taste of Home

Perfect for a special-occasion breakfast or holiday brunch; This easy egg scramble is a warm and hearty home food

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups diced peeled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped sweet red pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cups cubed fully cooked ham
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup 2% milk
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt

Steps

  1. Place potatoes in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Drain
  2. In a large skillet, saute half of the peppers and onion in 1 teaspoon oil until tender. Add half of the ham and potatoes; saute 2-3 minutes longer.
  3. Gently pour in the eggs. Pause to let them heat slightly.
  4. Use the spatula to move the eggs across the pan so they cook evenly
  5. As the eggs start to set, add chopped bits of ham or cheese
  6. Gently spoon the eggs onto the warmed plates
